Gaming consoles provide some of the most immersive experiences inside the entertainment industry. More so then movies and books due to the ability to directly affect the world and become a part of it. Video games are not anything new but have been contently improving raining in popularity and normality. Now most households have a gamin console or someone that plays on a pc. Modern games tend to have an immersive story and with graphics constantly improving it’s even easier to lose yourself inside a gaming world, but with vr you can actually enter it.
Virtual reality take this to the next level by placing you into the world rather than just letting you control it through an avatar. This gives the most immersion possible by connecting you to the games themselves, you raise your hand in reality and you raise your hand inside the VR world. The downside of this is that while virtual reality technology isn’t too recent its surge in popularity is, many people can get motion sickness and other problems however this is something that can be overcome by repeated use.
